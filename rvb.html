Equity vs. Invested Capital Growth
Home equity built (down payment + principal paid + appreciation) vs. investing the same capital
Home equity built = down payment + principal paid to date (proper amortization, front-loaded interest) + home appreciation above purchase price. Tracks wealth created through ownership without deducting the outstanding loan balance. Invested capital = your down payment + closing costs compounded at the opportunity cost rate — what that same money would grow to in the market.
